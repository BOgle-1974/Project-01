Chronic Kidney Disease

Hello! My name is Brian. Welcome to my page!
I created this site in the hopes of finding others in any stage of chronic kidney disease. I am currently in stage 4.
Like you, I am hoping to stop its progression and perhaps one day reverse it! In the following pages, I will explain
Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD), show you the advancements in conquering the disease, and then I will show you
where to find inspiration! Thank you!
